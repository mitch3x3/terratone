About

Our Mission

An art project at heart, terratone.io was created with the goal of mapping background sounds from all over the world. With the initial proof of concept recorded in San Francisco, our hope is to grow the size of our sound map via user submissions.

Contribute

We make it easy to and fast to contribute. Just navigate to the 'submit' page and follow the two step process of submitting your audio file. First submit the audio file to my dropbox account, and then fill out the submission form with the recordings details and coordinates. Audio files with the format wav or aiff are preferred, but mp3's are also accepted. All of San Francisco's sounds were recorded using a Tascam DR-40 with a deadcat filter for noise reduction due to wind. In order for submission to be considered the audio file must be in stereo and noise-free within reason. Each submission will be screened to meet quality standards.
